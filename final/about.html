Earl Clark

Senior at the U of M

After attending my local community college in Frederick, MD for two years and receiving an associate degree in general studies, I worked in apple factory for eleven months while researching higher education and applying to universities. Ultimately I decided upon the Univeristy of Montana since being accepted into the Wilderness and Civilization program.

My first semester here in fall 2017 was the most valuable semester of education in my life and my two years spent here have been action-packed. I've received a relentless education and I will be gradauting with a major in English with minors in Media Arts and Wilderness Studies.

Next year I will be starting graduate school at New Mexico Highlands University in Las Vegas, NM. I will be in the media arts program; eventually receiving a M.F.A. in Cultural Technology.
