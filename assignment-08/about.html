Why I chose this project.

I chose this project due to its simplicity. The form can be conveyed in three photos.

What it means for me.

The flying fish is a paper airplane model that I've never gotten to fly very well. At this point I'm not sure if its flight pattern is supposed to go immediately into a spinning dive, resemble a fish's swiming pattern, or whether the model is an inside joke in origami circles.
