Flying Fish

by Kyong H. Lee

Materials
6" by 7 3/4" origami paper
Pair of hands
Flat surface

Step One Step Two Step Three

- Fold paper in half and unfold.
- Fold the top corners inward to center crease and flip over.

- Fold the top point down to bottom of corner folds.
- Flip over and valley fold.

- Fold the left corner down to the base. Repeat on other side.
- Fold wings down and adjust to a 40o angle.
